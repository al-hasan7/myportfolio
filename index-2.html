About Me

Student Researcher

I am a Research Assistant at the Center for Research in Computer Vision (CRCV), UCF, working with Dr. Chen Chen. I am also affiliated with the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB), working with Dr. Zheng Zhang. I love exploring multimodal research that combines text, video, image, and audio.

In summer 2025, I've joined Meta as a Multimodal LLM Intern.

Earlier, I interned at Nokia Bell Labs in 2024, focusing on LLMs' Research. From 2023 to 2024, I worked as an Alexa AI Researcher and was the global 2nd place winner of the Alexa Social Bot Challenge-5, where we developed conversational agents, modeled long dialogue contexts, and generated images from chat inputs.

In 2022, I interned at Amazon Music as an Applied Scientist, working on music-related language models and monitoring systems.

Before that, I worked as an AI Scientist at Hishab Ltd. on Bangla NLP (2020–2021).

I completed my Master’s at Stevens Institute of Technology (2021–2023), focusing on NLP and deep learning.

I’m passionate about deep learning research and using AI to solve real-world problems.
